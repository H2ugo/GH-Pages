TRAILER

Figment is an puzzle-adventure game set in the human mind. Literally.
This particular mind has been quiet and calm for many years. But something happened, bringing up new thoughts and old fears that take the form of nightmarish creatures who spread chaos wherever they go.
The grumpy Dusty is called upon to get back to his old self and do his job as the voice of Courage: help the mind face its fears.
Join him and his ever-optimistic companion Piper, on an adventure through different parts of the mind, solving puzzles to navigate this surreal world and beating up nightmares.
