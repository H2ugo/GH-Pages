TRAILER (FR)

La Horde is an action game prototype freely inspired by the French author Alain Damasio's eponymous novel ("La Horde du Contrevent" // "The Horde of the Counterwind"—unfortunately not available in English to this day).
In a hostile world, shaped by a relentless wind, lead your Horde against the air currents towards the Far-Upstream, the presumed source of all winds.
