Official Music video for Gravity by Stöj Snak (feat. Sigurvin Sigurdsson).

Quite a different project than Jawtrap, but a welcome change. When Stöj Snak approached me with their upcoming EP, this song stood out for me, and I set out to make a video for it. Early on I managed to enlist Chloé Hallé to help, and she made the frame by frame character animations. 

Written, produced, played and recorded by Niels Højgaard Sørensen.
