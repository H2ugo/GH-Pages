Student project from 2016, which I had the privilege to ignite with the simple idea of adapting one of my favourite books. The pitch went through several rounds during the second half of 2015, before production could finally start with the full team. Also, the first proper game I've ever worked on.

OVERVIEW

Status: Prototype (2016)
Roles: Project Lead, Concept Artist & Game Artist
Team size: 15
ITCH.IO
TRAILER (FR)

La Horde is an action game prototype freely inspired by the French author Alain Damasio's eponymous novel ("La Horde du Contrevent" // "The Horde of the Counterwind"—unfortunately not available in English to this day).
In a hostile world, shaped by a relentless wind, lead your Horde against the air currents towards the Far-Upstream, the presumed source of all winds.
