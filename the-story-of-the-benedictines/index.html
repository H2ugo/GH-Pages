Graphic Novel - The story of the Benedictines

Mission:
Misc.

Original title: L'Histoire des Moines Bénédictins (FR)

A French comic book I illustrated some (!) years ago, long before I ventured into game art. 

In the middle of the 2016 summer, A good friend of mine, Thibault Neve, and I got commissioned to illustrate the story of the benedictine monks, written by Laurent Bidot. Over the course of these several months, I suspect I drew most of the monks I'll ever draw in my career. The head count eventually reached a few hundreds.
