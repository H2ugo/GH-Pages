Mission:
Key Art

View project

Key Art for the upcoming horror game Video Ghastlies.

Indie Publisher ORO Interactive approached me to help them create the key art for the exciting horror title Video Ghastlies.
Their pitch was to produce an image evocative of the 80s horror-film VHS covers. Hard to say no to that.

Beside the main artwork, I also delivered the variations designed to be integrated in Steam.
